about the classes

We offer classes for a diverse range of age groups, ensuring a lifelong engagement with the art of Indian dance. Young children, starting from age 3, are introduced to basic movements in a playful and nurturing environment. The 7-12 age group builds on this foundation with more structured lessons, developing technical skills and an appreciation for the cultural aspects of dance. Teenagers engage in more complex choreography and expressive techniques, preparing them for advanced performances. Adults of all levels, from beginners to seasoned dancers, are welcomed to refine their skills, embrace the joy of dance, and connect with a rich cultural heritage.

Content of classes

Children under 12: They start with the basics of dance in a fun and engaging environment, focusing on foundational Bharatanatyam. As they progress, older students in this group explore classical Odissi while also learning choreography that blends semi-classical Bollywood, Rabindrik, and Indian-inspired contemporary styles.

Teens and adults (Odissi): Classes focus on classical Odissi, complemented by advanced choreography in semi-classical Bollywood, Rabindrik, and Indian-inspired contemporary styles.

Teens and adults (semi-classical): These students follow a similar (choreography of semi-classical Bollywood and indian contemporary) path but without the classical Odissi focus.
