Age groups

We offer classes for a diverse range of age groups, ensuring a lifelong engagement with the art of Indian dance. Young children, starting from age 3, are introduced to basic movements in a playful and nurturing environment. The 7-12 age group builds on this foundation with more structured lessons, developing technical skills and an appreciation for the cultural aspects of dance. Teenagers engage in more complex choreography and expressive techniques, preparing them for advanced performances. Adults of all levels, from beginners to seasoned dancers, are welcomed to refine their skills, embrace the joy of dance, and connect with a rich cultural heritage.
