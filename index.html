Our first annual event

An Evening of Indian Classical and Semi-Classical Dance

Our annual events are always intended to be vibrant celebrations of Indian traditional dance and music. Our first annual show in the December of 2023 featured stunning performances from both the academy's talented students and guest artists. The audience was captivated by a rich array of classical and semi-classical dance forms, showcasing the beauty and depth of India's cultural heritage. This inaugural event successfully highlighted the academy's dedication to preserving and promoting Indian dance traditions while providing a platform for students to shine alongside experienced performers.
